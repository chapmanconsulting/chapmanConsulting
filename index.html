Who We Are

We are a non-profit, student-led organization that focuses on developing the next generation of top management consultants by giving students the opportunity to solve real-world business problems for our clients. Our aim is to provide our consultants with the unique experience of collaborating with a variety of organizations cross-industry while delivering high-quality work and value to our clients.

As an industry-oriented student group, we also focus on providing opportunities for students to succeed as competitive candidates for entry-level consulting jobs. We organize extensive training and professional development for our consultants that will well prepare them for a strong career in the top-tier consulting firms.

Chapman Consulting Group is one of the prime platforms that facilitates professional-level interaction between students and companies, while promoting information exchange and experience-sharing among students. Our primary goal is to help students excel and gain real-world experience during their time at Chapman.

Mission and Goals

Chapman Consulting Group seeks to implement and showcase a new business strategy in which organizations of any nature can stay economically viable not only in the short- and medium-terms but also in the long-term. We tailor this strategy to each of the individual organizations we consult with, shifting focus away from short-term and day-to-day operations and onto more fundamental aspects that shape a company’s long-term prospects.

CCG's goal is not only to create a competitive edge for our clients but also to create a social and professional atmosphere among our students and clients.
